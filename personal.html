Personal Information

 

I am from a beautiful country named Bangladesh which is located in South Asia. I was born in Chittagong which is a port city and also the second largest city in Bangladesh.

For my elementary education, I went to the Chittagong Collegiate School which is one of the oldest and most famous schools in Bangladesh. From this school I passed the S.S.C (equivalent to O-level) in the year 2000. After that I went to the Notre Dame College, Dhaka for my Higher Seconday Education. This is also one of the most famous colleges in Bangladesh. I passed the H.S.C (equivalent to A-level) from this college in the year 2002.

I am the only son of my parents and I have two sisters. My father is a journalist and my mother is a service holder in bank. My wife Saima Sultana Tithi is a Computer Science graduate from BUET and now a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science department at Virginia Tech, USA. My hobbies are solving programming problems, watching TV, reading blogs and of course eating various types of delicious foods.
